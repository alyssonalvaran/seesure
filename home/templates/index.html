SeeSure is a mobile application that allows patients with epilepsy manage their seizures and health better.

What It Does

The application is able to monitor the health condition of the patient through the data from the Seizure Diary and sensor data from a smartwatch. Using a specialized algorithm it also aims to detect and predict seizure occurrences. Once a seizure is detected, emergency mode will be activated.
